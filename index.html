FAQ

Questions?

What makes Kobrain different from ChatGPT or Claude?

Those live in a chat window. Kobrain lives in your workflow — your email, your repos, your calendar. It actually does the work, not just answer questions. And it's built custom for you, not a generic product.

How does the custom onboarding work?

You tell us what you need — what tasks eat your time, what tools you use, what you keep putting off. We configure your Kobrain around that. It's not a "here's the product, figure it out" situation.

What if it makes a mistake?

Kobrain asks before taking big actions. You set the guardrails — it stays within them. Like managing any teammate, you review the work before it goes live.

Can it really handle coding tasks?

Yes. Kobrain connects to GitHub, Vercel, and more. It can write code, review PRs, manage deployments, and handle the busywork that eats your day as a developer.

Is my data secure?

Kobrain only has access to what you grant it. You can revoke access anytime. Your data is encrypted and never used to train models.

When can I get started?

We're in early access. Join the waitlist and we'll reach out to get you set up. Early members get priority onboarding and founding member pricing.
